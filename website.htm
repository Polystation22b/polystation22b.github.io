Pokemon Aureus Red is a new and up and coming rom hack based on Firered/Leafgreen.
It will have many quality of life improvements. Many pokemon will have different types, like
Charizard being Fire/Dragon and Psyduck / Golduck actually being Psychic Type. The story
will take place 5 years after HGSS but only in the Kanto Region.
